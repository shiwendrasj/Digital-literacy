Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist:-
Do's:


Fact-check information before sharing or reposting news.

Think before you post — consider how content reflects on you professionally.

Enable Two-Fctor Authentication (2FA) on all social media accounts.

Separate personal and professional/academic profiles where possible.

Give credit when sharing others' work, images, or ideas.

Share projects, hackathon wins, and certifications regularly.

Don'ts:

Don't post photos or tag people without their consent.

Don't share personal information like your address, phone number, or daily schedule publicly.

Don't engage in "flame wars" or disrespectful online arguments.

Don't overshare personal struggles, conflicts, or sensitive life events publicly.

Don't plagiarize or repost content without proper attribution.

Don't post unprofessional content that you wouldn't want an employer to see.
